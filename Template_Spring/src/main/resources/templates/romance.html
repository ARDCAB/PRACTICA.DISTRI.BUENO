EMMA BY JANE AUSTEN

A witty and independent young woman,
Emma Woodhouse, delights in matchmaking
but refuses to fall in love herself.
However, her meddling often leads to mis
understandings, and she ultimately
learns that love is more complicated and
closer to home than she realized. A classic
comedy of manners and romance.

FUNNY STORY BY EMILY HENRY

Emily Henry’s latest novel likely follows her
signature blend of romance, wit, and emotional
depth. Centered around a protagonist
dealing with heartbreak, an unlikely romance
develops—perhaps in an unexpected or ironic
way—leading to a journey of love and self-discovery.

INTERMEZZO BY SALLY ROONEY

Since Sally Rooney has not yet released
Intermezzo, its plot remains unknown.
However, given her previous works, it is
likely to explore themes of love, class,
and existential uncertainty through the
lens of deeply introspective characters
navigating modern relationships.

ALL ABOUT LOVE BY BELL HOOKS

In this powerful nonfiction work, bell hooks
examines the nature of love—romantic, familial,
and self-love—through a feminist and philosophical
lens. She challenges traditional notions
of love, arguing that society’s misunderstandings
hinder true connection. A deeply
insightful and transformative read.

BIG SWISS BY JEN BEAGIN

Greta, a transcriptionist for a sex therapist,
becomes infatuated with a client she
has never met—Big Swiss. When the
two women meet by chance in real life, Greta is
drawn into a complicated relationship
built on deception. A darkly comedic
and thought-provoking novel about desire,
trauma, and identity.

A NOVEL LOVE STORY BY ASHLEY POSTON

After a painful breakup, the protagonist finds
solace in their favorite book—only to be magically
transported into its world. There, they
encounter the charming yet fictional love interest
and face the challenge of trying to stay in
the book's pages or return to reality. This
romantic, whimsical tale explores self-discovery,
healing, and the power of stories.

KEEPING 13 BY CHLOE WALSH

After everything they’ve been through,
Shannon and Johnny are still fighting
their battles—together. But as the weight
of their past threatens to pull them apart,
they must find the strength to hold on.
Shannon is trying to heal from the trauma
that haunts her, while Johnny, the rising
rugby star, struggles with the pressure
of fame and expectations. Their love has
survived so much, but will it be strong
enough to withstand the biggest test yet?

WHAT IF IT'S US BY BECKY ALBERTALLI & ADAM SILVERA

Arthur and Ben meet in New York City in a
moment of serendipity, but fate seems to
keep pulling them apart. As they try to
navigate the unpredictability of love and life, they
wonder: are they meant to be, or are some
love stories just not written in the stars?
A heartfelt LGBTQ+ romance about first love,
timing, and second chances.
